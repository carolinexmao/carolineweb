Miniatures (Mini-Model Houses)

1. Sweet Berries Time

This charming model of a quaint bakery was made over the course of 2 months, and required a lot of skill and prescision. Everything you see inside it was hand-made out of a large array a materials, many of which you can find inside your house. This bakery contains a cake display and one little cafe table, with many many plants and accessories added in. I did the lighting myself and had to use elctrical tape and lightbulbs to complete the wanted look and asthetic. I am very proud of this creation and hope you enjoy it too.

Stuffed Animal Creations

I made these cute stuffed animals over the course of 2 years. It started as a hobby and I slowly bought more materials and improved my sewing skills. I hand-sewed all of these without a sewing machine, and while I do not reccommend doing that, it helped improve my sewing skills over the years. I got all these sewing patterns from a great website, called "cholyknight". All of the pushies were made from free downloadable patterns on the website, except the unicorn, which was a sold pattern.

More on Miniatures

Making miniatures is a whole entire hobby, in which you get kits, or fully DIY out super cute, extremely detailed, and carefully designed miniature homes. While this hobby requires skills from sewing to wiring, it is still family friendly, and you can purchase simple kits that teach you the basics. The thing about this hobby is that you really have to put your heart into it so that you can make these beautiful creations. You have to work for the asthetic and really try to pull things together with your skill and talent. If you work hard, you will be awarded with a beautiful result.

More on Sewing Plushies

Sewing plushies is a great way to pass time and create something that you can sell or show to your friends. Sewing requires skill, and to coordinate the right shapes and pieces together to get the look and shape you want is hard. You need cloth, basic sewing skills, and most importantly, patience. It takes a while to get things right and if you make mistakes, you have to redo certain parts. Anyone who tried sewing really developes skills for making things among others. Sewing Plushies is a great hobby, and I reccommend it to anyone who wants to try it.
